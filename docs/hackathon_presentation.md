# MindLedger - Hackathon Presentation

## Slide 1: Title & Vision
**Title:** MindLedger
**Subtitle:** Turn Conversations into Actionable Insights
**Tagline:** Your Intelligent Meeting Knowledge Base

---

## Slide 2: The Problem
**"Information is lost in the noise."**

*   **Meeting Overload:** Teams spend hours in meetings, but key details are often forgotten.
*   **Lost Action Items:** "Who was supposed to do that?" becomes a common question.
*   **Retrieval Difficulty:** Finding a specific decision in a 1-hour transcript is painful and slow.
*   **Manual Effort:** Summarizing notes manually is tedious and error-prone.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution - MindLedger
**An AI-powered bridge between raw conversation and structured knowledge.**

*   **Automated Intelligence:** Instantly transforms transcripts into structured summaries.
*   **Centralized Knowledge Base:** One place for all meeting insights.
*   **Interactive Retrieval:** Don't search—just ask. Chat with your meeting history naturally.

---

## Slide 4: Core Feature - AI Summarization
**From Chaos to Clarity**

*   **Smart Parsing:** Takes raw text and extracts structure.
*   **Auto-Generated Outputs:**
    *   📑 **Meeting Outline:** High-level agenda tracking.
    *   ✅ **Action Items:** specific tasks assigned to owners.
    *   💡 **Key Takeaways:** Critical knowledge points and decisions.

---

## Slide 5: Core Feature - Interactive Knowledge Base Chat
**"Chat with your Data"**

*   **Context-Aware Q&A:** Ask questions about a specific meeting or your entire history.
    *   *Example: "What are the action items for John from last week?"*
*   **Rich Responses:** Supports Markdown tables, lists, and code blocks.
*   **Real-Time Experience:** Streaming AI responses for instant feedback.

---

## Slide 6: User Experience & Workflow
**Built for Speed and Usability**

*   **Seamless Context Switching:** Jump directly from a meeting detail to a chat session with context pre-loaded.
*   **Session Management:** Organize your research with multiple chat sessions (Create, Archive, Delete).
*   **Modern UI:** Responsive design with optimistic updates for a snappy feel.

---

## Slide 7: Technical Architecture
**Modern, Scalable, and Efficient**

*   **Frontend:**
    *   React 18 + Vite (Fast HMR)
    *   Tailwind CSS (Responsive Design)
    *   React Router (SPA Navigation)
*   **Backend:**
    *   Node.js + Express (RESTful API)
    *   Server-Sent Events (SSE) for Streaming
*   **AI Engine:**
    *   LLM Integration (MiniMax API)
    *   Prompt Engineering for structured extraction

---

## Slide 8: Future Roadmap
**Where we go from here**

*   ☁️ **Cloud Integration:** Migrate from local JSON to PostgreSQL/Vector DB.
*   Audio Transcription:** Direct upload of audio/video files.
*   📅 **Calendar Sync:** Auto-import meetings from Google/Outlook.
*   👥 **Collaboration:** Shared team workspaces and real-time editing.
